\section{Problem Formulation}
\label{sec:formulation}

Mathematical formulation of your problem. For example
\bea 
\min_{u(t)} & \displaystyle \int_{0}^T g(x,u) dt + h(x(T)) \label{eq:1} \\
\subject & \dot{x} = f(x,u)
\eea 

Equation \eqref{eq:1} can be referred by setting up its label.
